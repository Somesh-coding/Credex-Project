# GTM

## Target user

Engineering founders and early-stage startup teams spending money on AI tools every month.

## Initial distribution

* Share screenshots on X and LinkedIn
* DM founders discussing AI tooling costs
* Share results in startup and developer communities
* Ask founders to compare current AI stack costs

## Why this could work

The tool provides immediate value before asking for email capture.

The public share pages also create a natural viral loop because users can share savings screenshots publicly.
